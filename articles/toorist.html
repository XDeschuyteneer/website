Toorist @ UMONS

Toorist is an android project made for the University of Mons during summer 2012 with David Hauweele. The goal is to give to students an offline (totally) app to navigate in Mons and in the university. It's very flexible, extensible and based on open street map.

Don't enjoy it, owned by UMONS, closed source (but maybe one day...)
Try it: on Google Play
